AI Platform to Monitor Heart Health

The Platform analyzes ECG acquired from any single lead device.

The most common single lead devices are Apple watch (ECG enabled), Fitbit Sense, etc. The platform is device agnostic.Unlike many of the device providers, the platform diagnoses several types of arrhythmia conditions. The reports generated using Artificial Intelligence are accessible to general physicians and specialists. The platform also connects patients with specialists for consultation.
